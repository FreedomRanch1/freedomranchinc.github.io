About Us

Welcome to Freedom Ranch, Inc., a nonprofit equine-assisted therapeutic program offering by-appointment-only sessions in a quiet, farm-based setting. Our services support adults seeking personal growth, emotional regulation, stress reduction, and recovery-oriented experiences through structured, non-riding equine-assisted activities.

Our sessions emphasize personal insight and self-awareness, emotional regulation and mindfulness, confidence and trust-building, and connection, grounding, and reflection. All sessions are scheduled by appointment only to ensure safety, privacy, and individualized attention.

Important Notice: Freedom Ranch, Inc. provides equine-assisted therapeutic programming and is not a substitute for licensed mental health or medical treatment. Individuals seeking clinical or emergency services should contact a licensed provider or emergency services directly.

Our Services

Personal Growth Sessions

Structured equine-assisted activities designed to support self-awareness, personal insight, and individual development in a safe, supportive environment.

Emotional Regulation

Ground-based experiences with horses that promote mindfulness, emotional awareness, and healthy coping strategies for stress and emotional challenges.

Confidence Building

Activities focused on building trust, self-confidence, and interpersonal connection through meaningful interactions with horses.

Recovery Support

Recovery-oriented experiences emphasizing grounding, reflection, and connection as part of a holistic wellness journey.
